Feeding and Nutrition

Cats require a high-protein diet with essential amino acids like taurine. Provide fresh water at all times and avoid feeding them harmful foods like onions and dairy products.

Exercise and Enrichment

Cats stay active through play. Toys, scratching posts, and climbing trees help keep them engaged. Interactive play strengthens their hunting instincts and prevents boredom.

Grooming

Most cats groom themselves, but regular brushing reduces shedding and hairballs. Long-haired breeds require more frequent grooming to prevent matting.

Health and Vet Visits

Routine vet check-ups and vaccinations are essential. Watch for signs of illness like lethargy, loss of appetite, or changes in litter box habits.

Emergency Care

If your cat is choking, has difficulty breathing, or has ingested something toxic, seek veterinary help immediately. Keep a pet first aid kit on hand.
